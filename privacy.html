Corporate Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2025

1. Introduction

At SmashLabs, we are committed to protecting the privacy and confidentiality of our corporate clients, HR partners, and business users. This policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you engage with our services.

2. What We Collect

  • Business and contact information (company name, contact person, business email, phone)
  • Booking and inquiry details
  • Usage data (website analytics, device/browser info)

3. How We Use Your Information

  • To deliver and manage corporate wellness services
  • To communicate with HR and company representatives
  • To process bookings, payments, and invoices
  • To improve our offerings and client experience
  • To comply with legal and regulatory requirements

4. Confidentiality & Security

All business and personal data is handled with strict confidentiality. We use industry-standard security measures to protect your information from unauthorized access or misuse.

5. Your Rights

  • Access the information we hold about your company
  • Request corrections or updates
  • Request deletion of information (subject to legal requirements)
  • Opt-out of marketing communications

6. Cookies

We use cookies and similar technologies to improve your browsing experience. You can control cookie settings in your browser.

7. Contact

For privacy questions or requests, contact us at privacy@smashlabs.in or call +91 99999 99999.
